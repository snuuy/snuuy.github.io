CP Cheats

Third-party tools for manipulating player accounts on a popular MMORPG.

cpcheats.co
 Live
Visit Website
2013 - 2017

CPCheats.co was a website with various programs and services for the virtual world Club Penguin. It contained web apps that allowed users to connect to the game through our third-party client and manipulate their accounts, allowing users to obtain unlimited in game currency, virtual items, etc.

The website also had an arcade and prizes section, where users could play games and earn points which they could use towards purchasing Club Penguin membership gift card codes.

Over the years it was active, cpcheats.co amassed 22 million pageviews, 200,000 registered users, and earned $31,000 in revenue.

Tech stack: PHP, MySQL
